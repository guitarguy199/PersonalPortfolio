DEN FriendsCloudKit and MapKit in SwiftUI

DEN Friends is a social dining app designed for travelers, convention attendees, and weekend warriors to connect with their firneds and colleagues at the hottest restaurants in Denver. The app displays a curated map of restaurants, as well as a list view with detailed information including restaurant descriptions, navigation, and contact info. Additionally, the user can create a profile and check in to a restaurant, and see which of their friends have done the same - all in a fast, intuitive user interface.

The app was built in SwiftUI, and directly integrates MapKit for the location and navigation services. It also uses CloudKit to store information on the restaurants from a curated database, persist the users' profiles, and maintain checked-in/out status. It utilizes a mix of UIKit and SwiftUI components, and was architected in the MVVM design pattern.
