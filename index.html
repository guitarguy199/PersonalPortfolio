What I Do

iOS Development

With 3 years of iOS Dev experience, I have had the opportunity to learn and utilize many of the newest tools in both UIKit and SwiftUI, including: ARKit, RealityKit, CoreML, CloudKit, MapKit, Firebase, CoreData, and Realm. UX Design

I believe that an app will not succeed without a cohesive, well-executed user experience. My design philosophy revolves around engaging flows with smooth transitions, intuitive controls, and eye catching color schemes. My favorite tools include: Sketch, InDesign, and Photoshop.

Small Business Consulting

Through my freelance consulting company, Tangent, I have helped several small businesses identify financial, technological, and HR-related stopgaps in their growth. Past contracts have included restaurants, E-Commerce, Doctors' Offices and Surgery Centers.
